#Super Store Sales Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard using Power BI)
##Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Super Store sales data to understand business performance, customer segments, product categories, and profitability trends.
This dashboard helps business stakeholders track sales performance and gain data-driven insights to improve business strategies and increase overall revenue.

##Dataset Used
Super Store Sales Dataset
<a href="https://github.com/ragumass55/Super-Store-Analysis/blob/main/super%20store%20dashboard.png">Dataset</a>

##Questions (KPIs)
What is the total sales revenue?
What is the total quantity of products sold?
What is the total profit and profit margin?
Which customer segment contributes the most sales?
Which region generates the highest revenue?
How does sales trend change across different months and years?
Which product category generates the highest sales?
Which sub-categories contribute the most revenue?
Which shipping mode is used the most?
What is the sales and profit distribution by state?

##Process
Imported the Super Store dataset into Power BI.
Verified the dataset for missing values and inconsistencies.
Cleaned and transformed the dataset using Power Query.
Created calculated measures and KPIs for sales analysis.
Designed visualizations including cards, donut charts, line charts, bar charts, and maps.
Combined all visuals into an interactive dashboard with filters and slicers.
Dashboard

##Project Insights
The Consumer segment contributes the highest portion of sales.
Technology category generates the highest revenue among product categories.
Standard Class shipping mode is used the most.
Online payment method dominates compared to other payment modes.
Certain regions show higher sales concentration and stronger performance.

##Final Conclusion
The Super Store Sales Dashboard provides clear insights into sales performance, product categories, and customer segments.
By analyzing these insights, businesses can identify top-performing products, improve sales strategies, and optimize operations.
This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using interactive dashboards.
